Go Fish Rules

1. The game is played with a standard 52-card deck.

2. Each player is dealt 7 cards (5 cards if there are 4 or more players).

3. The remaining cards are placed face down to form a draw pile.

4. Players take turns asking a specific player for a specific rank of card.

5. If the player asked has the card, they must give all cards of that rank to the asking player.

6. If the player asked does not have the card, they say "Go Fish" and the asking player draws a card from the draw pile.

7. If a player collects all four cards of the same rank, they place them face up in front of them.

8. The game continues until all sets of four are collected.

9. The player with the most sets of four wins the game.
